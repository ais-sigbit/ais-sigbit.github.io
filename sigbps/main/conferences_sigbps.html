SIGBPS Workshop on Business Processes and Services (BPS'12)

 December15, 2012, Orlando, Florida, USA
Workshop Website: www.sigbps.org/bps12
(in conjunction with International Conference on Information Systems)

The purpose of this workshop is to provide a forum to discuss new research directions in the area of business process management and business service automation. A critical goal of the workshop is to cultivate high quality research concerning Internet-enabled business processes and services, which include new technological advances such as cloud computing, mobile commerce, service virtualization, and big data analysis.
